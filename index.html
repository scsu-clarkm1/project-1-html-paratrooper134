The Lazy Chef

How to make an Omelette for one with ease.

A picture of a cheese omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 Tbsp of butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Turn the stove on and heat a medium pan.
  2. Crack 2 large eggs and place them into a bowl.
  3. Place salt into the bowl as well.
  4. Scramble the eggs until they are a uniform liquid
  5. Spread the butter evenly into the pan.
  6. Pour the eggs into the pan.
  7. When partially cooked, add in shredded cheese.
  8. Fold the omelette into itself, trapping the cheese.
  9. cook until golden yellow.
